Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 is a vision for a future where innovation drives progress, sustainability ensures longevity, infrastructure supports growth, and empowerment uplifts every citizen.

Sustainability

Sustainability will ensure that Bangladesh 2.0 thrives by balancing economic growth with environmental protection. It aims to create a resilient future through renewable sustainable practices.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure will be the backbone of Bangladesh 2.0, supporting economic growth and development. It aims to build modern facilities that improve the quality of life for all citizens.

Empowerment

Empowerment will uplift every citizen by providing equal opportunities and resources. It aims to create a society where everyone can contribute to and benefit from the nation's progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation will be a vision of Bangladesh 2.0, creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Green energy will be a vision for Bangladesh 2.0, focusing on investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern Infrastructure will be a vision for Bangladesh 2.0, building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 is a vision for a future where innovation, sustainability, infrastructure, and empowerment come together to create a prosperous and inclusive nation. It is a commitment to leveraging technology and human potential to build a better tomorrow for all citizens."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
